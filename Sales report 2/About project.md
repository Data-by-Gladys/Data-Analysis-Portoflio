# Sales Data Analysis Using SQL

##Introduction
This project focuses on exploring and analyzing a sales dataset using SQL. The aim of the analysis was to understand the 
company’s overall sales performance, identify profitable product categories, and discover areas where the business may be 
losing money.

## Objective
The main objectives of this project were to:

a. Understand the structure of the dataset through exploratory data analysis (EDA)

b. Calculate total sales and total profit

c. Identify top-performing regions

d. Analyze sales and profit by product category

e. Determine the most profitable sub-categories

f. Identify orders with high sales values

g. Detect sub-categories that are making losses

h. Calculate profit margin for each category

## Methodology
The dataset was imported into a SQL database and several queries were written to explore the data, 
calculate key metrics, and generate useful business insights.


## Key Findings & Business Insights

The analysis calculated the company’s total sales and total profit to understand overall performance. By grouping 
sales by region, it identified the areas generating the most revenue. It also highlighted the top five most profitable 
sub-categories and major categories that earned over 10,000 in profit, such as Technology and Office Supplies. High-value 
orders above 1,000 were identified, showing their strong contribution to revenue. The analysis further showed that some products 
drive high sales but lower profit, while others generate strong profit, and that a few key sub-categories contribute most of the 
profit. It also revealed that customer segments spend differently and that the presence of many large orders suggests strong 
participation from business or high-value buyers. THe analysis also pointed out sub-categories were the company was losing money 
and the orders that contirbuted to these losses and as well as category with highest margin.

## Conclusion

The analysis shows that the company should review sub-categories that are making losses to understand possible issues with 
pricing, costs, or discounts. Regions with strong sales performance could be good areas for further growth and investment. 
Categories like Technology and Office Supplies should continue to receive attention because they contribute greatly to profit. 
Also, products with high sales but low profit should be examined to find out why they are not generating enough profit despite 
strong demand.
